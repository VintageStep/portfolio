This project ports one of the well-known Access and SQL-Server demo databases to Oracle Database. Featuring a fictonal company called Nortwind Traders. Its popularity comes from the fact that its data is well curated and as learning material is one of the best. No matter if you're just beggining with SQL, studying T-SQL(SQL equivalent of PL-SQL) or even learning to develop apps with data access, the data is robust enough to help you study what relational databases are capable of.

The motivation behind this project stemmed from my dissatisfaction with the demo schemas available in the version of Oracle Database that I was using. Although this was not the first time I attempted to find an alternative, previous attempts failed to consider the European regional characters stored in the original version. Additionally, they did not provide an installation procedure that followed best practices, such as assigning a dedicated owner and tablespace, or offering automated scripts to simplify the installation process. As a result, I decided to create my own scipts to address these issues.

Technologies used
